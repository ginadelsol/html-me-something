Cuba Libre!

A trip to the forbidden isle

Wanderlust

I like to travel. I enjoy seeing new geographic regions. I love experiencing new cultures, food, and arts. In August, 2015, I went to Cuba on a group tour and it was fascinating.

La Habana Vieja

Old Havana is a picturesque, historic section of Cuba's capitol. It is a must-see for anyone visiting Cuba. It has fabulous (often decaying) architecture, historic buildings, restaurants, shops and many strolling entertainers. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Visual Arts

Cuba is renowned for its music, and it also has a thriving arts scene. Visual arts were frequently on display in Havana and the surrounding area. One artist has his own miniature city not far from the center of Havana where he displays his mosaics on a grand scale.

Cars, Cars, Cars

Of course, no visit to Cuba would be complete without the old cars. American cars haven't been sold in Cuba since 1959, so it's not unusual to see vintage Chevies, Fords and Buicks tooling around the city. Many are used as taxis to cart tourists around.
